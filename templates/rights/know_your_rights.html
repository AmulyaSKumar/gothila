Know Your Rights

Fundamental Rights

  • Right to Equality (Articles 14-18)

    Equality before law and equal protection of laws

  • Right to Freedom (Articles 19-22)

    Freedom of speech, expression, assembly, association, movement, etc.

  • Right against Exploitation (Articles 23-24)

    Prohibition of trafficking and forced labor

  • Right to Freedom of Religion (Articles 25-28)

    Freedom to practice and propagate any religion

Legal Rights

  • Right to Legal Aid

    Free legal services for eligible individuals

  • Right to Fair Trial

    Right to be heard and defended by a legal practitioner

  • Right to Information

    Access to information under government control

  • Consumer Rights

    Protection against unfair trade practices
