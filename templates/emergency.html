Emergency Legal Help

If you are in immediate danger, call 112 (Emergency Services) immediately.

Emergency Helplines

  • Police: 100
    For immediate police assistance
  • Women's Helpline: 1091
    24/7 women's emergency helpline
  • Child Helpline: 1098
    For children in distress
  • National Legal Services Authority: 1516
    Free legal aid services

Immediate Actions

  1. Ensure your immediate safety
  2. Contact emergency services if needed
  3. Document everything (take photos, save messages)
  4. Contact a legal professional
  5. Inform trusted family/friends

Safety Tips

  • Keep important documents in a safe place
  • Save emergency numbers on speed dial
  • Document any evidence or incidents
  • Build a support network
